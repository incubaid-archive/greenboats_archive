Catamaran Safety and Comfort

Green Boat 1 is a revolutionary catamaran that is designed for safety and comfort: a lighter and the lower the point of gravity is safer when the boat sails through heavy sea and wind conditions and provides stability. Our 54 feet catamaran is very spacious for 8 passengers without loosing speed and performance at sea.
